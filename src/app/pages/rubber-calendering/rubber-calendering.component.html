Rubber Calendering

Rubber calendering is a process where rubber is passed through rollers to form sheets or films of specific thicknesses and sizes. This method is ideal for producing high-quality, consistent rubber products used in industries such as automotive, medical, and manufacturing. Calendering ensures precision, durability, and uniformity, making it perfect for custom rubber sheets, films, and components for diverse applications.

Custom Calendered Silicone & Rubber Sheets: Precision Thicknesses and Widths

Solid silicone and organic rubber sheets calendered down to 0.003" thick and up to 52" wide. We have 3 rubber calenders all with heating and cooling to process a wide variety of rubbers from silicone, to FKM, SBR, NBR and much more. Our equipment is setup to automatically cut to width, cut to length, or provide in continuous full roll dimensions. We can supply uncured or cured sheets, with single or double layered with polyethylene sheeting.
