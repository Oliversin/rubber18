Compounding

Rubber Compounding is the process of mixing raw rubber with various additives to create a custom material with specific properties like strength, flexibility, or heat resistance. By carefully selecting and combining elastomers, curing agents, and fillers, we produce high-performance compounds tailored to meet the unique needs of your application.
