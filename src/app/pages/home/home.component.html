Why Rubber-Mexico?

For over 50 years we have solved the most challenging manufacturing challenges. With our team of 650+ people including chemists, and engineers we are well prepared to execute well on a wide spectrum of parts. We are vertically integrated with compounding both organic rubber and silicone in house, and have a wide spectrum of manufacturing capabilities that often complement each other. We are one of the largest rubber molders in Mexico with experience making a wide array of parts from large floor mats to tiny electrical connector seals. We are also fully setup for rubber + silicone extrusions. We can extrude customer profiles, foamed/sponge rubber, fabric braided reinforced hoses and much more. We also make formed fabric reinforced silicone hoses for industrial and automotive applications. We are experts in making FKM/FVMQ lined silicone hoses for turbo charge and intercooler applications.
