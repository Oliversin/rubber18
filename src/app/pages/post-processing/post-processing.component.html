Post Processing

Post processing is an essential phase in manufacturing that enhances the functionality and appearance of products. Our advanced techniques and skilled team ensure that every item receives the attention it needs to meet rigorous quality standards. With a focus on precision and efficiency, we deliver tailored solutions that elevate your final products. Explore how our post processing capabilities can add value and enhance your production outcomes.

Prepping Parts

Once your part is rotomolded, we enhance its functionality and aesthetics through a range of post-processing services, including precise routing, thorough pressure testing, flame treating for durability, ozone treating for enhanced resistance, foam filling for insulation, and expert polishing for a flawless finish.

Assembly

For parts requiring fittings, stickers, or additional assembly, our dedicated team expertly manages the process, ensuring every component is seamlessly integrated for a polished, ready-to-use product.

Quality Assurance

We conduct rigorous quality checks throughout the post-processing phase to ensure every part meets your specifications. Our commitment to quality guarantees that each product is reliable, durable, and ready to perform at its best in any application.
